How To Play: Click on the origami images to earn points. If you get enough points, you will unlock
new origami that give more points.There are also auto clicks that you can buy that show up in the red rectangle.
If your points go below the required amount to unlock an origami image, you won't lose the image. You can
reset your game by pressing the Reset Game button.
